ABOUT ME

Good Day! Welcome to my personal page. I am Reign Cherubin Barongrong but you can just call me "CHE". Here is my website where in you'll see my past activities and some of my own works, especially when it comes to photography. I believe that art is a powerful language, capable of transcending boundaries and speaking directly to the soul. My work is an expression of showing my aesthetic as it is, weaving together elements of lines, structures and patters. Each piece is a photos that tells a story, provokes thought, or captures a fleeting moment in time.

Artistic Vision

In every digital pixel, I strive to create an immersive experience that resonates with the viewer. My art is a celebration of being free to capture moments in life and an exploration of the beauty found in the ordinary.
